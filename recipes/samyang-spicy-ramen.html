You will need:

  • One pack of Samyang Ramen
  • 5 cups of wooder
  • a pot or pan that can hold the wooder

STEPS:

  1. Add 5 cups of wooder into the pan/pot and add it to the stovetop. Set that mothafucka to high heat until it's boiling.
  2. After the wooder boils, open the pack of Samyang and add the ramen in the wooder. Lower heat to medium-high if ya want. Stir ramen in boiling wooder for a good 5 min.
  3. After 5 min, strain out da ramen from the wooder
  4. Add the ramen to a plate(or you can leave it in da pan/pot).
  5. Open up the spicy sauce pack and add it to the ramen. Stir it until the sauce is evenly spread out through the ramen.
